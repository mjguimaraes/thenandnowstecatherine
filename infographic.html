Collecting facts and stories about Ste Catherine street is like digging in a treasure chest amidst a room full of artifacts. It was, and is a street full of energy. From being a simple street connecting farm to farm, to an entertainment district, to a major commercial thoroughfare and home to many businesses, it has always been an important corridor to Montrealers and its visitors.
